The Face Behind The Facts

Sanket Upadhyay

"Journalism is printing what someone else does not want printed; everything else is public relations."

With over a decade of experience across India's top newsrooms, Sanket Upadhyay has built a reputation for calm, relentless, and unbiased journalism. He stepped out of traditional broadcast media to build an independent platform where news is driven by truth, not TRPs.

Career Highlights

  • Former Consulting Editor & Prime Time Anchor at top networks.
  • Extensive ground reporting on military, politics, and social issues.
  • Founder of Double Check, championing independent digital journalism.
